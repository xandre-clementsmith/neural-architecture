# Overview
### Info
- Limbic - Associative/Cognitive - Sensorimotor Gradient System from ventromedial outwards to dorsolateral
- Direct pathway with disinhibition (2 GABA signals), and indirect pathway with dis-disinhibition (3 GABA signals)
	- NOT XOR: Across 120 neurons, 37% were indirect, 3% were direct, and 60% were direct with indirect collaterals
