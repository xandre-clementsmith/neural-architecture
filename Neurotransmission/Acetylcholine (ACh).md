### Receptor Distribution
- Nicotinic / Ionotropic
	- A cys-loop ligand-gated ion channel member, alongside nicotinic [[Acetylcholine (ACh)]], [[Glycine]], [[Serotonin (5-HT)]] 5-HT3, and Zinc-Activated Receptors
	- Types
		- I: α9/10
		- II: α7/8
		- III: 
			- 1: α2/3/4/6
			- 2: β2/4
			- 3: β3, α5
		- IV: α1, β1, δ, γ, ε (Muscular)
- Muscarinic / Metabotropic
	- M1: Gq
	- M2: Gi
	- M3: Gq
	- M4: Gi
	- M5: Gq
